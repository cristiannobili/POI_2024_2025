Description

Created by three high school students, POI (Point Of Interest) is an interactive platform that connects Shakespeare's works to real-world locations.

The map allows users to discover historical details about the setting of each play.

Every point of interest includes both textual and visual content, making the discovery of the plays more interesting and intuitive.
