Home

Created by three high school students, POI (Point Of Interest) is an interactive platform that connects Shakespeare's works to real-world locations. The map allows users to discover historical details and analyses about each setting. Every point of interest includes textual and visual content, making the exploration of the plays more interesting and intuitive.

Luca Avveduto, Simone Cecire, Simone Cinquetti
